Hej, I am Håkan

I'm a DJ from Stockholm, and my musical journey has been an exciting and transformative experience. Growing up in the city, I was drawn to the dynamic energy of electronic music—the beats and rhythms that seemed to flow through every corner of the scene. It was in my teenage years that I first encountered a track that sparked an undeniable passion within me to create and craft my own sound.

I began by mixing tunes in my room, experimenting with a range of genres and techniques. Before long, I found myself playing at local clubs and events, where I quickly built a name for my high-energy sets and smooth transitions. My performances are a mix of deep house, techno, and progressive beats, each laced with my own creative twist.

There’s nothing like seeing the crowd fully immersed in the music, feeding off the energy that a live DJ set creates. Every track I choose is handpicked to take the audience on a journey, moving them from reflective, melodic moments to euphoric, high-energy climaxes.

Sweden's vibrant music scene has had a profound impact on my career. Influences like Avicii and Swedish House Mafia have set the bar high, and I'm proud to be part of this legacy. For me, being a DJ goes far beyond just playing tracks—it's about connecting with people, creating unforgettable moments, and sharing the unifying power of music.
