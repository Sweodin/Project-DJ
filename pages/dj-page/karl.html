Hej, I’m Karl

I am DJ from Stockholm, and my journey into the world of music has been nothing short of electrifying. Growing up in Stockholm, I was always captivated by the pulsating beats and vibrant rhythms of electronic dance music. My passion for music began in my teenage years when I first heard a track that instantly made me want to create my own sounds.

I started out by mixing tracks in my bedroom, experimenting with different styles and genres. It wasn’t long before I was spinning at local clubs and events, building a reputation for my energetic performances and seamless transitions. My sets are a blend of deep house, techno, and progressive beats, all infused with my unique touch.

I love seeing the crowd lose themselves in the music and feel the energy that only a live DJ set can bring. Every track I play is carefully selected to create a journey, taking the audience from mellow, introspective moments to high-energy peaks.

Sweden’s rich music culture has been a huge influence on my work. Artists like Avicii and Swedish House Mafia have paved the way, and I’m honored to follow in their footsteps. For me, being a DJ isn’t just about playing music; it’s about connecting with people, creating memories, and spreading the joy that music brings.
